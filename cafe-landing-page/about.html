About Us

From our coffee farms to your cup, we're committed to serving the best quality coffee. Our journey from seed to roast creates a unique flavor experience. Learn about our coffee journey to appreciate its rich taste.

NewPugs was founded in Seattle in 1971. In 1987, Howard Schultz bought Starbucks with local investors and opened the first international store in Vancouver. Today, Starbucks is the world's largest coffee roaster and retailer. Howard Schultz focuses on quality and customer service. He sources the best global beans to offer excellent products in comfortable spaces. NewPugs Coffee has become the world's leading premium coffee brand, praised by experts as the "Coffee Kingdom Legend."

Our mission at NewPugs is to inspire and nurture people's spirits through every cup and community. We strive for excellence and put our values into practice. Through each cup of coffee, we bring a unique NewPugs experience into customers' lives.
